Welcome to Agricultural Development Investments Limited (ADIL) Ltd, a company Founded in 2021, our mission is to enhance Malawi’s socio-economic landscape by boosting agricultural production and value addition.

Advisory services

ADIL shall provide advisory services to livestock farmers to improve their knowledge and skills in livestock production.

Veterinary services

The focus on veterinary support can lead to advancements in animal health practices, potentially reducing disease outbreaks and improving overall herd health.
